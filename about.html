About Fall 2020 Opportunities

Don't spend hours searching for something to do. Find the right opportunities here.

Due to COVID-19, many recent graduates and students are looking for Fall 2020 opportunities as schools move online and jobs are postponed. While other job sites exist, they are hard to navigate for only fall 2020 positions and do not offer other opportunites such as volunteering, non-profit, and teaching opportunites.

This is the sentiment I've heard from my close friends, classmates, Linkedin feed, and Slack channels. Currently, several companies have postponed their start dates including: Linkedin BLP: July 2021, Deloitte Consulting: September 2020 - January 2021 (Varied), Oliver Wyman: April 2021, McKinsey & Company: September 2020-November 2020 (Varied) ,Bain & Company: October 2020 (Varied), Boston Consulting Group (Varied), and Credera: October 2020

For the recent graduate, whose full-time offer got cancelled. Now is the chance, to pivot into your dream role. For the current university student, who is feeling uneasy about another semester on Zoom or cannot attend in-person classes.

For the non-profit, looking to find someone who is passionate about your cause. For the startup, looking to hire someone in the fall. For the organization, looking for someone to teach data science workshops monthly. For the university, looking for someone to help with reasearch. For anyone, looking to connect with talent for fall 2020. Fall 2020 Opportunites is for all of you.
